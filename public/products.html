Products

Because of our relationships with many suppliers, we can offer a wide range of products or provide bespoke products as required

LED & Electrical Products

LED battens with or without PIR or microwave movement sensors and automatic switch off, suitable for industrial, warehouse, commercial and domestic use. Many size and wattage available. Other products available including intruder alarm systems, electrical components, etc. Contact us for further details.

Commercial & Industrial ESS

We provide Energy Storage Systems for a range of industries, installers, construction and end users. Sizes can vary from c. 40kW/40kWh to >200kW/500kWH systems, with or without dedicated PCS. Systems usually comprise of solar strings, inverter, battery storage and management software, all set up to your own requirements. Can be combined with EV chargers, UPS Systems for Data Centres, etc, on-grid or off-grid for micro-grids.

Specialist Lithium Ion Batteries

Specialist lithium ion batteries are available for various uses including lighting columns, telecoms base stations, etc. Batteries for lighting columns are tall and thin for column applications available at various power configurations. Batteries for telecoms are usually 48v configuration and constructed to be mounted vertically or in rack formation. Contact us for more details.

Mobile Power / Mobile Energy Storage

Mobile energy storage systems 5kW - 10kW, 10-30kWh battery, including PCS, DC-DC converter, transformer, fire control system, configurable distribution board and energy management system. Battery can be extended with an additional pack, and can accept solar power for charging. Contact us for more details.

Solar Modules

Various sizes and power between 420 W and 480 W for standard half cell TOPCON modules <2m2 . Hampshire Imports are authorised distributors of Sunova / Thornova products (Tier 1 solar products) in the UK and these represent excellent value and quality products. Larger sizes and power panels are also available. Lightweight panels for use on low strength rooves and flexible panels are available for RV, caravan and marine applications. Contact us for more details.

Lithium Ion Batteries

We provide a range of lithium iron phosphate (LiFePO4) batteries and cells. We prefer LiFePO4 batteries due to their stability, safety, ESG benefits (no cobalt, long life and recyclability) and ease of use for stationary energy storage. We expect sodium ion batteries to be available in 2025 but for now only lithium ion are available. We can provide any size and configuration but 12v, 24v and 48v (50Ah - 200Ah) are popular. Battery uses include recreation, campervans, marine use, manufacturing, mobility, telecoms and many others.

Inverters & Charge Controllers

A wide range of inverter types, power and specifications are available. Solar inverters, hybrid inverters, bi-directional inverters, solar charge controllers and wind turbine charge controllers are all available for commercial and industrial scale uses, typically 40kw to >200kW. Send us your required specification and we will find a range of options for you.

EV Chargers & Cables

Residential and commercial EV chargers, 7kW to 150kW are available. From basic smart chargers to those suitable for commercial situations with RFID, app, web and smart card activation. Contact us for more details.

Solar Cable, Safety Cut-offs, Isolators, etc

Solar cable by the drum 4mm2 -> 10mm2, fire safety cut-offs, DC & AC isolators are all available. Contact us for more details
