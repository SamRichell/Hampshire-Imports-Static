About Us

A family business putting customers first.

We are a family business based in the UK, China, EU and South America specialising in importing from China for any products but with a focus on renewable energy technology, components and low energy products.

Language, business, and cultural difficulties of sourcing & importing from China can be quite a barrier – which is why we do it for you! We make the link to China’s suppliers and factories and we bring the products to you.
